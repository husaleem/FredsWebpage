Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bonjour Montclair brings the authentic flavors of Paris to the heart of New Jersey.

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, many sourced from local farmers who share our passion for quality.

At Bonjour Montclair, we strive to create an atmosphere that feels like a cozy Parisian café.
